About Us

Augsburg Air Transport (AAT) is a virtual airline founded with a strong passion for aviation and flight simulation. Based at Augsburg Airport (EDMA), we offer our pilots a realistic and professional environment to experience regional and executive flights across Europe and beyond.

Our mission is to provide high-quality flight simulation experiences with a focus on regional aviation, professionalism, and community. We welcome pilots of all experience levels ‚Äì from newcomers to real-world aviators.

Our Fleet

Beechcraft King Air 350

The King Air 350 is the workhorse of our fleet. With twin turboprops, it offers excellent performance, cabin comfort, and is ideal for short to medium-haul IFR operations throughout Europe.

Pilatus PC-12

Our PC-12 serves small airports and special operations. Its single-engine efficiency and rugged versatility make it perfect for executive and utility flights alike.

Cessna Citation CJ4

The CJ4 enables us to offer business jet operations with a blend of luxury and speed. We operate this jet on long-range charter and VVIP routes from Augsburg to destinations across Europe and North Africa.

Cessna 172 Skyhawk

The classic trainer. Our Cessna 172 is used for VFR flying, flight training, and local scenic flights. Perfect for beginners and relaxed GA flights under visual conditions.

Operations

We simulate realistic airline procedures, including:

  • Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
  • Dispatch Briefings & Route Planning
  • Dynamic Scheduling & Charter Missions
  • SID/STAR procedures, real-time weather integration

Our main hub is Augsburg Airport (EDMA), and we regularly operate to Munich, Frankfurt, Vienna, Innsbruck, Zurich, and many other regional fields. Our network expands through both scheduled and ad-hoc charter flights.

Our pilots enjoy autonomy and flexibility, while still benefiting from structure and realism. We promote flight logging, event coordination, and ATC interaction with the highest standard.

VATSIM & IVAO

We are an active participant in both the VATSIM and IVAO networks. Augsburg Air Transport encourages frequent online flying with ATC engagement. Our pilots are provided with callsigns, liveries, and resources to participate confidently.
