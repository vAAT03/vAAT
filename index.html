Our Fleet

Beechcraft King Air 350i (D-IMML)

The King Air 350i is our powerhouse turboprop – versatile, reliable, and elegant. Whether it’s a short regional hop or an alpine charter, this twin-engine beauty delivers unmatched comfort and performance in all weather conditions.

Cessna Citation CJ4

Speed, range, and style. The CJ4 is perfect for long-range corporate flights or precision approach training. With cutting-edge avionics and high-altitude capability, it’s an essential part of our operations.

Cessna 172 Skyhawk

Our classic training and light touring aircraft. The Skyhawk is trusted around the world and is the perfect aircraft to explore the skies in a relaxed and steady manner.

Cessna Citation CJ3+

The Citation CJ3+ is the perfect aircraft in our fleet for fast and efficient regional operations. Known for its speed, reliability, and sleek cabin, it is ideal for VIP charter and business flights across Europe and beyond. Featuring cutting-edge avionics, low operating costs, and great short-runway performance, the CJ3+ is a versatile jet that connects cities in record time – all with comfort and elegance.

About Us

Augsburg Air Transport is a virtual airline built for aviation enthusiasts who want something different. We focus on quality flying and immersive virtual experiences without trying to mimic the real world. No overly serious real-ops, no mandatory group flights – just pure flying enjoyment. Whether you're a GA pilot, a business flyer or just looking for laid-back flights in Europe, you're welcome here.
