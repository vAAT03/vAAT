Our Fleet

About Us

We’re not your typical virtual airline. At Augsburg Air Transport, we fly because we love it. No real-world ops, no rigid procedures – just chill flights, stunning views, and fun missions in MSFS2020, MSFS2024, and X-Plane. Whether you're practicing IFR in the CJ4, sightseeing in the 172, or cruising above the clouds in a CJ3+, there’s a place for you with us.
