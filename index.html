About Us

Founded by passionate flight simulation enthusiasts, Augsburg Air Transport is a German-based virtual airline dedicated to simulating realistic, small-scale commercial and executive air transport. Our community is focused on providing high-quality, immersive flying experiences.

Our mission is to bring together realism, professionalism, and community in virtual aviation. Whether you're a student pilot or a seasoned simmer, AAT offers you the platform to grow and enjoy structured, authentic flying.

Our Fleet

Beechcraft King Air 350

A twin-turboprop legend, used for our primary operations across Germany and neighboring regions. The King Air 350 is the perfect aircraft for IFR charter and short-haul flights.

Pilatus PC-12

The PC-12 supports our flexible single-engine operations to smaller airfields. Great range and efficiency with single-pilot capability.

Operations

We operate out of our virtual hub at Augsburg Airport (EDMA) and simulate real-world procedures including SID/STAR usage, realistic fuel planning, and crew briefings. Our schedules include:

  • Regional flights across Germany, Austria, Switzerland
  • Charter missions and event flights
  • Training flights with active ATC participation

Our pilots enjoy freedom in flight planning while following real-world standards. We provide liveries, dispatch briefings, and flight logs through our internal systems.

VATSIM & IVAO

Augsburg Air Transport proudly flies on both VATSIM and IVAO networks. Our official callsign is "AUGSBURG" with ICAO code AAT. We promote high-quality virtual aviation and coordinated flights with online ATC.
