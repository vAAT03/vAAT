Welcome to AAT

At Augsburg Air Transport, we bring a new level of passion and professionalism to the world of virtual aviation. As an enthusiastic and tightly-knit community of flight simulation pilots, we aim to create unforgettable flying experiences within the VATSIM and IVAO networks. Whether you're here to explore, train, or just enjoy aviation to the fullest — welcome aboard!

Our airline stands for more than just flying. We value precision, realism, camaraderie, and a shared passion for the skies. With a growing fleet of meticulously managed aircraft and a realistic operational environment, AAT offers a unique space for both beginners and seasoned sim pilots.

Our Fleet

Citation CJ4 (D-IAAT)

A high-performance business jet designed for speed, comfort, and range. Perfect for longer executive flights and VATSIM events where prestige meets precision.

King Air 350i

The backbone of our regional fleet. With exceptional reliability and versatility, the King Air 350i is ideal for shorter hops and scenic missions.

Cessna 172 (D-EERH)

The world’s most iconic trainer aircraft. Perfect for VFR flights, pattern work, and learning the fundamentals of flight with us.
