About Us

Augsburg Air Transport (AAT) is a virtual airline created with heart, detail, and a drive for realism. Our mission is simple: to make your time in the simulator meaningful and immersive. Based in Bavaria, Germany, our operations mimic real-world business aviation and regional transport, allowing you to experience authentic routes, airfields, and procedures.

From VFR circuits over the Alps to international IFR missions with the CJ4, our virtual airline is as diverse as the sim world itself. AAT offers a close-knit pilot community, helpful resources, and true structure for simmers who want more than just free flights.

"Here at AAT, every takeoff has purpose – and every landing has pride."

Our Fleet

Citation CJ4 (D-IAAT)

The CJ4 is the jewel in our crown. A sleek and fast business jet, capable of cruising FL450 while covering routes from Augsburg to Helsinki, Lisbon or anywhere in between. Ideal for IFR proficiency and business-style charter operations.

King Air 350i

For those medium-range flights with a classy, turboprop feel. Our King Air 350i excels in both performance and comfort. From mountain strips to busy regional airports, it's the pilot’s workhorse.

Cessna 172 (D-EERH)

The soul of general aviation. Whether you’re on a VFR cross-country or perfecting your landings, our C172 is a nod to flight training and weekend sky adventures. Loved by students, instructors, and sightseeing pilots alike.

Why Augsburg Air Transport?

  • ✅ Official ICAO Callsign: AUGSBURG recognized on VATSIM and IVAO
  • ✅ Real-world inspired dispatch, routes and schedules
  • ✅ Interactive pilot logbook & weekly route assignments
  • ✅ In-house liveries for MSFS2020 & regular fleet updates
  • ✅ Supportive pilot community via Discord
  • ✅ Monthly group flights and ATC events

From preflight to landing, AAT enhances your sim experience with structure, personality and real connection.
