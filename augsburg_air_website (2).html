About Us

Augsburg Air Transport is a virtual airline based in southern Germany, focused on regional air transport and executive charter operations. We prioritize short flights, small airports, and realistic simulation on both VATSIM and IVAO.

Our Fleet

Beechcraft King Air 350

Our primary workhorse for short regional missions. Efficient, STOL-capable, and perfectly suited for IFR operations across Europe.

VATSIM & IVAO

We are actively flying on both VATSIM and IVAO. Our official callsign is "AUGSBURG" with ICAO code AAT. Our pilots follow real flight plans, use proper phraseology, and engage actively with the virtual aviation community.
